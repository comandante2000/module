Self-Evaluation

1.Article I. section 3.

    An Electrical Engineer should strive to upgrade his skill and knowledge in electrical engineering in order to give the best possible service to the public which could be attained by interchanging information and experiences with other engineers, and by unselfishly contributing to the mission of engineering societies, institutes, schools and engineering and scientific media, and other public institutions.

2.Article II.

    An Electrical Engineer should be fair, impartial and reasonable in rendering professional service to his clients, employers and contractors regarding contracts or other agreements.

3.Article III.

    An Electrical Engineer should endeavor at all times to protect the engineering profession collectively and individually against misrepresentation, distortion, misunderstanding or unfair criticism. An Electrical Engineer should give credit for the engineering work to the person whom the credit is legally due.
